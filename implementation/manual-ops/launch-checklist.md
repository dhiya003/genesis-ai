# Manual Launch Checklist

Owner: Founder / Genesis Operator
Mode: Phase 1 manual execution, no paid infrastructure required

## Before publishing

- Confirm one offer, one price range, one target customer, and one lead channel.
- Complete competitor notes for at least 10 similar sellers or products.
- Prepare product promise post, how-it-works carousel, proof reel, and FAQ story.
- Create a manual lead tracker with name, source, need, price response, status, and next follow-up.

## Launch day

- Publish the promise post with a DM/comment call to action.
- Add the FAQ story and pin ordering instructions.
- Reply to every enquiry manually and record it in the lead tracker.
- Capture objections exactly as customers write them.

## Daily validation

- Count qualified DMs, serious enquiries, paid orders, and repeated objections.
- Update the launch pack assumptions when real customer evidence contradicts them.
- Decide continue/change/stop after 7 days.
