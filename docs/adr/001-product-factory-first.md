# ADR-001: Product Factory First

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Genesis AI has a long-term BusinessOS vision. Building the full platform immediately would create too much scope and cost.

The current phase must stay lean and usable without paid infrastructure.

## Decision

Build Genesis Product Factory first.

The first product will create practical launch packs from founder requirements and support manual execution in Phase 1.

## Consequences

Benefits:

- Faster delivery
- Lower cost
- Clear MVP boundary
- Early business value
- Cleaner automation later

Tradeoffs:

- Some steps remain manual
- Live integrations are deferred
- Wider BusinessOS modules come later

## Review Trigger

Review after the first end-to-end Product Factory workflow is working.
