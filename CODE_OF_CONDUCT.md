# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Genesis AI is a serious engineering project. Contributors are expected to act with professionalism, respect, clarity, and accountability.

## Expected Behavior

- Use respectful language.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.
- Document decisions clearly.
- Give and receive feedback constructively.
- Prioritize long-term maintainability over shortcuts.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Disrespectful communication.
- Publishing secrets, credentials, or private data.
- Making undocumented architecture changes.
- Claiming work is complete when it is not.

## Enforcement

The Founder and maintainers may remove, reject, or revert contributions that violate project standards.

## Genesis Principle

> Human strategy. AI execution. Documentation always.
